Always. Be. Running.

PROJECT
Always. Be. Running.
ROLE
Front End Development & Design
TOOLS
HTML, CSS, JQuery, Mapbox
OVERVIEW
Always. Be. Running. is a personal project that contains a ledger of all the races that I completed ever since I started running after university. I was inspired to get into running from Casey Neistat's YouTube movie "Intensity" and thought that having a history of all my races would allow to me track my progress and motivate me to continue running in the future. Moreover, this mini-site was an opportunity for me to improve my front-end development skills and explore new tools such as Mapbox .
