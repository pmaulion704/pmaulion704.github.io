Overview

[UPDATE]: Featured in inVision's — Studio Smashers Vol. 6

After seeing Gleb Kutnetsov's 'Simple logo motion' on Dribbble, I was inspired by their short animation brainstorms and decided to create a challenge out of it - "Create a different brand animation for 30 days straight". I used this challenge to work on my animation skills and to push myself outside of my comfort zone. All of my short brand animations were created via inVisionStudio. At the end of the day, I really enjoyed this challenge and often found myself pondering how logos could potentially be connected together via motion design.
