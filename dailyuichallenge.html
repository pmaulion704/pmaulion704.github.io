Overview

To some it may seem as a rite of passage for many designers in the Dribbble community, I decided to take on the Daily UI Challenge, a 100 day challenge to create something new from scratch. I used this challenge to explore new patterns, color palettes and expose myself to as many different areas as possible, from different devices and screen sizes to incorporating interactive shots with animation. The main tools I used for this project were Sketch and InVision Studio. Over the 3+ months, the challenge was a great outlet to put aside the daily patterns I saw at work and dive into something new and test my creative ability.
